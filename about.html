About

I'm Ajayi Caleb Oluwapelumi a frontend software developer

Programmer & Graphics Designer

My journey in tech was quite funny and interesting. I am this kind of person that like trying things. I was privileged to go through an NCE where I started with Agricultural science during my Pre-NCE days, I later changed to Computer/Mathematics at my NCE 1 and finished my programme with a very good results. Before my NCE days, I went for a computer training of which I obtained my Diploma certificate in computer. After 3 good years at a home, I got admitted into a University to Study Computer Science but unfortunately we went on ASUU strike/Corona break and fortunately for me, during the break I was introduced to HTML where I started my tech journey. My knowledge then was so primitive as my instructor didn’t even knew much about what he taught me in HTML then. When we resumed back, I joined a coding group in school known as Google Developers Group where I obtained in-dept knowledge in HTML and also in CSS. When it was time for my SIWES, my eyes were more opened to the tech world and I was able to lay my hands on different things such as HTML, CSS, JAVASCRIPT, MySQL, Cordova and so on. I even built some working Android applications such as a CGP calculator amidst other. I am versatile in Photoshop, Corel-Draw, Microsoft Office Suites and many more. Today, I am still in tech learning new things to improve my skills in coding.
